The Profile Optimizer skill rebuilds every section of your LinkedIn profile around a single conversion goal. It produces three headline options for you to choose from, a human-written about section structured for trust and conversion, rewritten experience entries in narrative format, a featured section strategy with two high-value links, and four fully-formed image generation prompts for your banner, profile picture, and featured section tiles. Everything outputs in code blocks ready to copy straight into LinkedIn.

<Note>
  If `about-me.md` exists in your project, the skill reads it and pre-fills your name, audience, topics, and point of view before asking any questions. It will tell you what it pulled.
</Note>

## Trigger phrases

```
optimize my profile
fix my LinkedIn
rewrite my headline
profile review
LinkedIn audit
rebuild my profile
```

The skill also triggers when you upload a LinkedIn profile PDF or screenshot for review.

## Prerequisites

No API keys required. Image generation prompts are output as text for use with Gemini — no API access needed. `about-me.md` is optional but speeds up input gathering significantly.

## Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather inputs">
    The skill collects your primary LinkedIn goal, main offer type, brand colours, and social proof in the first batch of questions.

    **Goal options:** Booked calls, inbound leads, newsletter subscribers, or job opportunities

    **Offer options:** Coaching, consulting, agency services, or freelance

    A second batch asks for the external links you want in your featured section and how to access your current profile (paste it, start fresh, or upload a screenshot).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write three headline options">
    Three headlines are written and output in a code block. You then pick one.

    **Constraints:**
    - Maximum 50 characters each
    - Sentence case only (not Title Case)
    - No job titles ("Founder", "CEO", "Designer")
    - No filler words
    - Format: [Core value] + [for target audience]

    The three options cover different angles:
    - **Direct:** outcome + audience
    - **Pain-focused:** problem + audience
    - **Differentiator:** unique angle + audience
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write the about section">
    Output in a code block. Structure: Hook → Struggle/Empathy → Method/Philosophy → Authority → CTA.

    Full sentences throughout. No forced line breaks mid-sentence — LinkedIn handles text wrapping on mobile and desktop. Double line breaks between sections. Tone is punchy, direct, and human.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rewrite the top two experience roles">
    Output in a code block. Each role follows the structure: Context → Challenge → Action → Result.

    Storytelling format, not bullet points. 8–15 sentences per role maximum. No "Managed a team of…" or "Responsible for…" constructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Featured section strategy">
    Two items maximum. Both must be external links.

    - **Item 1:** Primary conversion goal — direct path to main offer (booking page, application form, sales page). Title: 3–5 words, benefit-focused.
    - **Item 2:** Secondary value builder — build trust or capture leads (newsletter signup, free resource, portfolio, case study). Title: 3–5 words, benefit-focused.

    No subtitles. No internal LinkedIn posts. No "DM me" items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visual design brief">
    Four image generation prompts, each in its own code block and fully self-contained for Gemini.

    Brand colours are stated at the top of the brief with a one-line explanation of why they fit the positioning.

    After all four prompts: "Copy each prompt into a new Gemini chat one at a time. For the banner and profile picture, attach your headshot alongside the prompt. The featured tiles do not need a photo."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Rules and constraints

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Headline rules">
    No job titles in headlines. Maximum 50 characters. Sentence case. Lead with core value, not your role or company name.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Copy formatting">
    All profile copy (headline, about, experience) must be in code blocks. Write full sentences — do not force line breaks mid-sentence. LinkedIn handles wrapping on both mobile and desktop.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Featured section">
    Maximum two items. Both must be external links. No internal LinkedIn posts, no "DM me" items, no subtitles.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Image prompts">
    Each prompt must be fully self-contained. The user pastes it cold into Gemini and gets the asset. No prior context required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Scope">
    The skill ends at the visual design brief. It does not offer to write a launch post or engagement strategy after delivering the profile.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
